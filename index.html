Welcome to RiTUAL !

We are a multidisciplinary research group based in Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) focused on advancing the state of the art in NLP. Our team combines expertise from various domains to tackle complex scientific challenges and publish high‑impact research.

Our Research Areas

Pushing the boundaries of NLP through innovative, socially-aware research

Code-Switching and Multilingual NLP

Building inclusive NLP through code-switching and multilingual research. Our research covers multiple sub-areas including code-switching, multilingual understanding and cross-lingual transfer, especially on under-represented languages.

Multimodal Learning and Benchmarking

Developing multimodal systems that integrate vision, language, and other modalities for richer content understanding. Our research spans vision-language models, video understanding, visual question answering, theory of mind, and constructing comprehensive evaluation benchmarks.

Affective Computing and Content Analysis

Understanding human expression and moderating online content through NLP and multimodal analysis. Our applications include emotion recognition, sentiment analysis, misinformation detection, content safety assessment, and narrative theme interpretation.
